About

Hello there! I'm Ellie! My birthday December 31st, 2020 and I'm an English Lab. I've had a few families since I was born, but I finally made it to my forever home! I'm really smart and have learned a ton of tricks already! My favorite is whichever one will get me a cookie. My favorite toys are sticks, squeaky plushies, and tennis balls I can catch! I love going on walks, playing in the pool, and daycare. The only things I don't like are carrides, but mom feeds me treats during them so it's getting better.
