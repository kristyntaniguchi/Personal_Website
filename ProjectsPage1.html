Maximus

About

Born on January 4th, 2015, Maximus (Max for short) is a 6-year-old Plott Hound mix. Originally from Oakland, CA, he was flown up to Seattle when he was 3 months old and was adopted from Seattle Humane Society on May 16th, 2015. Max enjoys long naps on the couch, food, walks, food, daycare, food, and did I mention food? His dislikes include loud noises, being left alone, and getting wet.
