Lucy

About

I AM LUCY! I'm a black lab and I was adopted on October 24th, 2019 from RASKC in Kent, WA. My mom and dad found me running along the side of the street one night and took me to the shelter so my family could find me. The nice people at the shelter called my first parents, but they decided to let my MOM and DAD take me home instead. Now I have 3 sisters and 2 brothers to play with all the time. One of the best things about this house is the food! It's so good that it makes me dance before every meal time. I also have a million tennis balls, squeaky toys, and chew toys to play with. The only thing I wish I could do is play with the new chickens mom just got, but she says I'll scare them. Maybe one day.
